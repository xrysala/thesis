Teachers must be able to browse as well as create topic or course with the possibility to add and manipulate material or resources as per need.
Teachers must be able to easily create exercise on a selected topic or course.
Teachers must be able to add and manipulate file resources with respect to specific course or a topic.
Teachers must be able to create flash cards.
The students must be able to browse and search a topic in any domain.
Students must have the ability to write notes and could also take printouts of them.
The students must be able to create flash cards and share them among others.
The students must be able to do some exercises.
The systems must be able to show results at the end highlighting score.
